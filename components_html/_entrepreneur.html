EquilTech GmbH

A medtech company based in Ulm, Germany, to fight shoulder and neck pain.
To do so, we were developing an AI high-tech sensor which detects un-physiological posture.

We iterated towards an optimal business model.
As CFO my responsibilities were budgeting, financial forecasts / monitoring and financing.

My further tasks were: Building the business model, strategy and organization, pitching to investors and customer acquisition (B2B). In the end, I could attract a 200K financing.
