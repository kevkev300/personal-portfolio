AirLoan

AirLoan is a peer to peer money lending platform for connected users who wish to borrow small amounts of money for a short period. It is hassle-less and has unbeatable interest costs. The website includes a reservation dashboard as well as a map search.

Shuffle

Shuffle is a mobile first web app for young and versatile job-hunters who wish to connect with companies in a matter of minutes. All without the need of a CV.

This app was built with tinder-like UX and a complex matching algorithm.

Cocktail 'n' Mix

A hobby app to create, save and find your favorite cocktail. Check it out! No email required.
